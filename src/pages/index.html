My name is Marjorie Amrom. I have been hosting guests for over 30 years and enjoy meeting people from all over the world. I am a world traveler and would love to share my extensive art and antique collection with you. It would be my pleasure to act as your personal concierge and help you enjoy what my wonderful city of Philadelphia has to offer.

Get in Touch

Location

Centrally located at the corner of 10th and Lombard Streets, in a safe upscale area of center city and easily reached by car, train, bus or plane. Built in the early nineteenth century, my home is historically certified and is furnished with art and antiques from around the world. You will be within easy walking distance to the Convention Center, Liberty Bell and Independence Hall, Pennsylvania and Jefferson Hospital, Magic Garden, theater, great shopping and much more. There are many wonderful restaurants and cafes a few steps away which will suit all tastes and budgets.

Amenities

All rooms contain free WiFi, cable TV, small refigerators, microwaves, coffee makers, telephone and much more. Each unit has it's own heat and air-conditioning so guests may adjust the temperature for their comfort. In addition, your room will be stocked with continental breakfast foods (coffee, tea, juice, bottled water, yogurt and cereal). Dining table, dishes, etc. are also supplied so that guests may help themselves at their convenience. Rooms and bathrooms are cleaned on a daily basis. There is metered and timed on-street parking (for visits of two nights or more the city offers visitor's parking permits for $15, must be purchased in advance, email for details), as well as a comercial public parking garage one block away.
